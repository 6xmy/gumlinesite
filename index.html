How it works

Up and running
in 30 seconds.

1
Add the bot
Invite Gumline to your server — no configuration required out of the box.
2
Set your call channel
Type g.setup in the channel you want to use. Only needed once.
3
Start a call
Type g.c and Gumline matches you with a random server in seconds.
4
Just chat
Messages, reactions, GIFs, stickers — everything syncs live. Use g.s to skip or g.h to hang up anytime.

Last updated: March 2025

What We Collect

Gumline stores only the minimum data required to operate: server (guild) IDs, configured channel IDs, and custom command prefixes. This is stored locally on the bot's server and used solely to provide the service.

We do not store message content, user IDs, usernames, profile pictures, or any personally identifiable information beyond what Discord makes visible during an active call session.

Message Relay

During an active call, messages are relayed in real time between two servers. This relay is transient — messages are not logged, stored, or retained after they are sent. Once a call ends, no record of the conversation exists on our systems.

Third Parties

Gumline does not share any data with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade any information. The bot operates entirely within Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.

Data Removal

To remove your server's data, simply kick Gumline from your server. All associated configuration data is automatically deleted. For questions, reach out in our community server.
